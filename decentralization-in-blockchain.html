What Is Blockchain

Blockchain is a public ledger that keeps the digital ledger in chronological order which is immutable and secure. In other words, we can say that it is a collection of a distributed assortment of immutable infrastructure code which is linked and secured with a fingerprint.

It is therefore made accessible to the public on the internet. The blockchain solution is trending all over and hence it is a demanded technology at present.

Before making use of blockchain technology it is very essential to understand how blockchain works and what are the blockchain technology fundamentals. In this way, you will be able to make use of them in the easiest way.

Mainly blockchain consists of three concepts namely block, nodes and miners. Here every chain consist of multiple blocks and each block has three elements.

It is necessary to understand the basics of blockchain so that you can make use of the technology.

There is data in the block.

A 32-bit whole number called a nonce and is randomly generated when a block is created.

Next is the hash which is a 256-bit number and wedded to nonce.

What Are The Different Types of Blockchain?

Blockchain is known to be a wonderful innovation at present time and has a wide range of distribution option. We have already given clear information about what is blockchain infographic and now the next is what are the different types of blockchain?

There are four different types of blockchain namely-

Public

A public blockchain is known as a different type of blockchain technology which is defined as the permission distributed ledger technology. In this type of blockchain, anyone can join and perform a transaction and each peer has a copy of the ledger. Also, anyone can access the public blockchain if they hold a web connection.

Uses are in the case of voting and fundraising.

Private

This type of blockchain works best in the restrictive environment which can also be defined as the closed network. Besides this, it is a permissioned blockchain that is under the control of an entity. They are best suited to be used by a privately own company or an organisation that wants to use them for internal cases.

They are primarily used in supply chain management, asset ownership and internal voting.

Hybrid

Hybrid is a combination of public and private blockchain type and it works like a use case in an organisation. It neither wants to deploy a private or a public blockchain rather than deploying both.

There are many industries to which blockchain technology has made its reach. Here are a few of them-

The most important use of blockchain technology can be used in case of money transfer from one part to another. When it comes to the transaction with banks which can take up to 24 hours but when it comes to blockchain technology it can be done in second. Now you can figure out how beneficial the technology is.

Healthcare

Health data that is covered up in blockchain includes age, gender and the medical history of an individual. In this case, without any concern or the secured key, no other person will be able to access the information of any other.

Real Estate World

In case of purchasing a property, you have to make certain documents or we can say paperwork is a necessity which contains title. So when it comes to blockchain technology, it can stores the titles which is needed at the time of buying and selling on the blockchain network while allowing a clear and transparent picture of legal ownership

Insurance

The greatest use of blockchain technology in the insurance industry is through contracts. Here the contracts allow the customers to manage and claim them in a secured manner. Later all the contracts and claims is being recorded on the blockchain validated by the respective network where the invalid claims are being removed.
