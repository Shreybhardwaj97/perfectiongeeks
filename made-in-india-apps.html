Indian app development industry

These are the gorgeous Indian mobile applications that make you proud feel on the Indian app development industry

Earlier the future predictors had said that India will rise as a world power in the upcoming time and that their words will go true very soon. The high-level participation of India in the growth and adoption of technology is itself declaring the shimmering future of Indian app development industry. In the same aspect, the listed mobile application developed in India is assumed as the fabulous growth of India in the technology world, especially in the mobile app development field.

So, today everyone knows that though its App Store or Play store, there are a number of mobile applications that are performing wonderfully and also have a huge download count along with amazing reviews and feedback for clients. Do you know that the mobile applications for which we are talking about are developed in India itself? If no… then, here is the list covering all the astonishing high performing mobile applications developed in India, and definitely it will make you feel proud on the Indian web app development industry.

1. Flipkart: An E-commerce shopping cart app

Withholding a 4.5 rating, along with millions of downloads and client reviews, Flipkart is a top-performing and majorly used e-commerce mobile application developed in India. It’s a shopping cart mobile application that is developed to enhance the online shopping experience of the Indian audience. Additionally, to fetch users with every shopping requirements, the application believes to offer everything for online shopping, like Electronics, Clothing, Groceries, Appliances, Beauty products, Accessories, Footwears, Toys, Sports items, Gift cards, Ticket bookings, Furniture, and much more. Along with this, the Flipkart app also trusts to introduce and run a number of exciting discounts offers on the shopping to enthral and attract more users.

The app layout, error-free access nature, well-categorized product listings, multilingual feature, quick assistance and many more in-built features and functionalities make it a top-rated Indian mobile app.

2. Myntra: A shopping app

This is yet another mobile application developed in India which allows the Indian audience to shop online for the multiple ranges of dresses though it's Men, Women or Kits outfits, the app has every category of designer outfits in several sizes that excites you more to shop. In addition, to provide a better shopping experience, the Myntra app is designed on a color layout format along with several smoothly accessible features like add to cart, view more you like, recently viewed items, similar products, separate categories, easy to access filters and more. Meanwhile, in addition, to grab more attention from the nationwide users the app believes to offer to win insider points feature, in which users can get a chance to win exciting insider reward points on shopping from the app every time.

This is considered as the most gorgeous Indian developed a mobile application which is performing amazing by providing a marvellous shopping experience to the users in an easy manner, and hence the app is having 4.2 ratings on the play store.

3. Zomato: Online food ordering app

It’s a majorly used Indian developed app which allows users to order food online from their favourite restaurant. Zomato app is holding a 4.2 rating on the play store and mind-blowing reviews from the users. To make it easy for users to order their favourite food from the favourite restaurant within their locality the app is finely developed on the format where users can easily select a restaurant and view their entire menu along with the menu item name, price, description and quantity. Additionally, to make the application a bit more user friendly, to get the food of their own taste and spice level, the application has an inbuilt feature which allows users to suggest additional requests about spice level to the restaurant of their order.

Apart from that, the app is integrated with an easy payment method which allows the users to pay for their order via using any particular digital payment method.

Swiggy, Uber, Faasos are also doing good in the online food ordering industry, and these applications are also developed in India.

4. Paytm: Money transferring app

India is moving toward its bright future by adopting all the aspects of digitization, and the Paytm application is the best instance of this. This is a most trustworthy mobile application developed in India which simply grants its users to transfer money directly from bank account to personalized Paytm wallet and other purposes like shopping, recharge and all. Paytm is one of the best Indian mobile applications that has the highest download rate as of 350 million as of the last year survey.

Apart from a number of exciting and smoothly accessible features this app also believes to offer splendid cash backs and discounts every time to engage more users. The app is conceptually developed in a fully safe way, therefore all your data (personalized bank details) will be safe and therefore the app gains the highest rating of play store as 4.5 stars.

5. Hike Messenger: Chatting app

Twinkling with a 4.4 rating on the play store the Hike Messenger is an Indian mobile application which is developed with an intention to deliver an amazing brother less chatting or messaging experience to the national audience. Alongside this, the app is a joyous bundle of multiple features like emojis, stickers, etc, that a user can send with a message to express their feeling in an elegant way, and much more.

6. Quikr: Buying and selling app

Holding a 4.3-star rating with thousands of wonderful client feedbacks, Quikr is an Indian mobile application which grants users to classified their used and fresh products to sell in the niche market. Additionally, to sell products using the Quikr app, all the users are first requested to be a verified member on the app and then they need to list their selling items within the app along with the required item price, description, and image.

To interact one user to another to discuss the product as its cost and everything the app comes along with an in-built instant chat feature where users (buyer and seller) can get connected and discuss the deal in a written format.

7. NewsHunt: Reading app

Newshunt is a wonderfully developed Indian mobile application which is specially designed by keeping the reader in the mind to provide them with a facility to read their choice of content online or also on a single touch, whether its news, blogs, or ebooks. To cover every sector audience, this app trusts to deliver both paid and free reading services.

8. RedBus: Online Ticketing app

Earlier the ticket booking process consumed a lot of time while planning to travel anywhere, and to heal the Indian people with this issue, the developers have invented the red bus app. This is an awesome Indian mobile application which permits users to book online bus tickets in a most convenient way using this mobile app. Along with online bookings, this app also allows its users to choose the bus seat of their own preference as the front seat, back seat, window seat etc. The app is connected with the huge network of bus travellers of moreover 2500 bus operators across the nation.

Navigating the boarding point using the real-time location track feature helps the app to become the first choice of users in concern to book tickets online.

9. Ola cabs: Taxi app

This app covers the largest taxi network across India. Ola is an Indian developed mobile application that allows users a facility to book an auto or a car to travel within the city and also to outside the city. The application has come along with a live tracking system with which users can easily check the current location of their booked cab. Likewise Ola Cabs, Jugnoo Autos is another taxi app developed in Indian and performing excellently along with holding a 4.2 rating on the play store.

10. Book my show: Ticket Booking app:

This is a charming Indian mobile application which successfully wins users' hearts by allowing them to book a movie ticket online without compromising their comfort zone. Well, in addition, to make the user feel excited to use it, the app is not just limited to movie ticket booking, as it allows users to book tickets for every other event happening in the city like pays, concerts, and else.

Apart from online ticket booking for a show, the app also has an in-built feature to watch the show trailers, feedback, etc, about the particular show/movie.

11. Hotstar : Live streaming app

This is no doubt the top-rated Indian mobile application, which is working awesomely in the entertainment industry by granting individuals to watch movies, TV shows, Match, News, etc,, in several languages, so that the users with any particular native spoken language can enjoy the app in their own preferred language. This is the most popular and loved Indian live streaming app.

12. Gaana: Audio streaming app

Listening songs is the best remedy to get rid of boringness, having this thought in mind the Indian developers have developed and launch this wonderful audio streaming app, which covers moreover 3 million latest and old songs that are finely filtered in several categories like bhajan, Bollywood songs, romantic hits and more, in addition, to make it easy for the audience to run their favourite playlist.

13. In short: News reading app

This is one of the marvellous Indian mobile applications which makes the individuals updated with the latest and trendy worldwide news. Instead of showing the time consuming long news entries and stories, this app’s motive to make you aware about the world wide happening in a short manner, so as the app covers and shows the basic and focal points of the news.

14. IRCTC Connect: Indian Railway app

This is the most useful Indian mobile application maintained and organized by the Indian government. The app is designed with a purpose to make individuals enable to book a train ticket, check PNR status, birth availability, train location or status online on a single platform in just a few seconds.

15. Map My India

This is an awesome Indian mobile application, which is also the best alternative for Google Maps in India that offers digital map data, telemetric, location-based SaaS and GIS services.

So, now you know how wonderfully India is performing in web app development as the above listed are the 15 best mobile applications developed in India that you must have to download and use and have to be proud upon.
